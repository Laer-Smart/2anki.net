## The single biggest gap

Paid acquisition has been in a sustained four-week collapse, not a one-week dip. New paid conversions: 29 → 12 → 5 → 1. MRR -14.1% from its April peak; active paying subs -127 in 7 weeks (886 → 759). The `ads_conversion_Success_Page_1` event fired 80% less often; Stripe success-page referral traffic -52%. The Day Pass webhook was misconfigured for several days (users 19848 and 21605 paid and never unlocked), and the only external Auto Sync trial ever (user 19374) bounced on a silent-zero result. This is not a top-of-funnel problem — signups -50% is the symptom. The conversion engine is healthier than ever (99.5%, +45%). We cannot reach 300 K by improving conversion further; the gap lives on the scale leg of the goal, and it is now expressed as a payments-trust and one-shot-retention problem.
