## Priority shift for the next 7 days

The user-count goal and the churn problem share one root: 2anki is a one-shot tool, and we don't even keep an account for most of the people it serves. The highest-leverage move needs no new traffic — **spec and ship in-flow signup capture at the moment of a successful anonymous conversion**: "Your deck is ready — save it to My Decks" with one-tap account create on the download screen. It directly grows the user count the goal measures, converts traffic we already have (6% baseline, measurable in a week), and an account is the precondition for any return/habit loop that addresses the "I don't use it enough" churn. Keep the conversion-fidelity fixes flowing (they're plausibly what stabilized subs this week), but pause net-new SEO/landing expansion until in-flow capture ships.
