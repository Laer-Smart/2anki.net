# Sign in with Apple

Sign in with Apple lets you create an account or log in using your Apple ID — no separate email or password needed.

## Finding the button

The Apple button is currently available on `/login?apple=true` and `/register?apple=true`. Add `?apple=true` to the URL if you do not see it. It appears as the third option in the provider row, between Notion and Microsoft.

## First sign-in

The first time you sign in with Apple, you choose whether to share your real email or use Apple's Hide My Email. Both work with 2anki.net. If you share your real email, your name is also captured and stored on your account. If you use Hide My Email, Apple forwards our emails to your real inbox automatically.

## Returning sign-ins

On subsequent visits, tap the Sign in with Apple button and authenticate with Face ID or your Apple ID password. You land directly in your account — no email or password entry on 2anki.net.

## Hidden email

If you chose Hide My Email, transactional emails (deck-ready notifications, password reset) are routed through Apple's relay. Delivery works the same as with a regular email address. You cannot change your hidden address from within 2anki; if you want to use your real email instead, contact support@2anki.net.

## Adding your name later

If your name was not captured at sign-in (for example, if you had already authorised this app under a different context), you can update it in account settings.
