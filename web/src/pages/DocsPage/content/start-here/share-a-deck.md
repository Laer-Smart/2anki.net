---
title: Share a deck with a link
description: Turn any converted deck into a link anyone can open — no account needed.
---

Open a converted deck in **Deck preview** and click **Share** in the top-right corner.

2anki generates an unguessable link. Anyone who has the link can:

- Preview all the cards in a browser
- Download the `.apkg` to import into Anki

They cannot edit the deck, and they do not need a 2anki account.

## Stop sharing

Open the preview page, click **Share**, then **Stop sharing**. The link stops working immediately. You can create a new link at any time.

## Privacy

The share link is not indexed by search engines and does not appear on 2anki.net. Only people you send the link to can reach it.

Be careful with decks that contain personal notes — anyone with the link can read the cards.

## Downloads page

Decks with an active link show a **Shared** badge on your downloads list.
