title: See your study stats on the Ankify page

The Ankify dashboard shows live study stats pulled straight from your hosted Anki. You see them without opening Anki itself.

**Plan:** Available to Auto Sync subscribers and Lifetime members. The stats live on the Ankify dashboard, between the conflicts banner and your synced pages.

## What you'll see

- **Reviewed today** — cards you've studied since your Anki day rollover.
- **Day streak** — consecutive days with at least one review. A day with no reviews yet keeps yesterday's streak alive until the day ends.
- **Reviewed this year** — total cards reviewed in the trailing 12 months.
- **Review heatmap** — one cell per day for the last year, shaded by how many cards you reviewed that day. Hover a cell for the exact count and date.
- **By deck** — a stacked bar per synced deck showing New, Learning, and Review cards waiting for you.

## When stats don't show

Stats are read live from your hosted Anki each time the page loads. If the container is offline, the section stays calm and tells you it will load when Anki reconnects — usually within a few minutes. Nothing is lost; refresh once it's back.

Only decks 2anki synced for you appear in the by-deck bar. Decks you created by hand in Anki aren't tracked here.

If a deck or count looks wrong after a study session, see [When sync gets stuck](/documentation/sync/troubleshooting).
